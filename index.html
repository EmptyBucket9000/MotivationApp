Motivation: Achieve Your Goals

This is the support page for my motivation mobile app.

Please contact me with any feature requests or bugs. You don't need to leave your name or email if you're not interested in hearing back from me.

For bugs, please first make sure you've updated the app as it's possible the bug has been fixed already. If that doesn't work, please continue below.

If you're submitting a bug report, please provide the following:

  1. Your device type (Android, iPhone, iPad)
  2. Your operating system version
    • iOS
    • Android
  3. As much information as you can give about what you were doing in the app when the failure occured

Thank you!
